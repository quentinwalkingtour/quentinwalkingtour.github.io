How To Visit Paris? 🤓

For precise recommendations, feel free to text me or contact me at quentinwalkingtour@gmail.com

🗼 Must-See in Paris
  • Eiffel Tower / Notre-Dame / Louvre : try to go early in the morning!
  • Montmartre – Check my website for a list of spots to visit (in the right order 🙂).
  • Quartier Latin: Panthéon, Luxembourg Garden, small streets, student area.
  • Le Marais: Place des Vosges, Hôtel de Ville, Jewish quarter, small medieval streets, Hotel Sully.
  • Île de la Cité: Notre-Dame, Sainte-Chapelle, Conciergerie, bridges.
🏛️ Check Out a Museum
  • The 14 "Museums of the City of Paris" are free – check the list online!
  • Art – Petit Palais (free!) / Orsay Museum (Little Louvre) / Musée de l'Orangerie
  • Centre Pompidou / Fondation Louis Vuitton – great modern art museums.
  • Musée de la Préfecture de Police (Police Museum)
  • Musée des Égouts (Sewer Museum)
  • Musée du Fromage (Cheese Museum 😋)
🥐 Food experience
  • Bouillons (several in Paris) – authentic Parisian restaurants, simple food, very affordable. Often crowded on weekends. Also look for brasseries everywhere – open all day.
  • French crêpes on Rue du Montparnasse.
  • Baguette sandwich from any good bakery (no need to search Instagram – good Google ratings are enough!).
  • Vietnamese food in Belleville ⭐ (metro). I love Dong Huong, but many great places.
  • Falafel in Le Marais (many on Rue des Rosiers).
  • The Paris Mosque ⭐ serves couscous and amazing pastries.
  • Coffee and bakery Arab pastries at the Paris Mosque / Café terrace anywhere in Paris
🧺 Food Market

Walk in a food market and make your own picnic: bread, cheese, fruit, wine (mostly mornings/lunchtime, schedules vary).

  • Marché des Enfants Rouges ⭐ (more like a food court with amazing options!).
  • Marché d'Aligre
  • Marché Saint-Quentin
  • Marché Saint-Germain
🍸 Nightlife
  • To have a drink: Le Marais – many wine bars and cocktail places / Canal Saint-Martin – relaxed bars along the canal / Saint-Germain-des-Prés – classic Paris cafés
  • Party: Oberkampf ⭐ / Pigalle / South Pigalle
☀️ Where to Relax or Enjoy the Sun?
  • Place des Vosges ⭐
  • Jardin des Plantes / Luxembourg Garden / Parc des Buttes-Chaumont
  • Père Lachaise Cemetery ⭐ – many famous graves and stories.
  • Bois de Vincennes – rent a boat on Lac Daumesnil.

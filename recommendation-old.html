My Paris Recommendations 🤓

Everything a local would tell you over coffee

⭐ = personal favourite — don't miss it
  • Eiffel Tower / Notre-Dame / The Louvre — go early in the morning!
  • Montmartre — curated list of spots on my website, in the right order 🙂
  • Quartier Latin — Panthéon, Luxembourg Garden, charming small streets

Tap any pin to open in Google Maps

  • The 14 "Museums of the City of Paris" — always free, check the list online
  • Petit Palais — free, beautiful permanent collection, never crowded
  • Musée de Cluny ⭐ — medieval art in the Quartier Latin, free under 26
  • Musée Rodin ⭐ — intimate museum with a lovely garden, the opposite of the Louvre
  • Musée d'Orsay — Monet, Renoir, Van Gogh. Enough said.
  • Musée de l'Orangerie — Monet's Nymphéas in a stunning circular room
  • Centre Pompidou / Fondation Louis Vuitton — top modern art
  • Curiosities: Police Museum / Sewer Museum / Cheese Museum 😋

Tap any pin to open in Google Maps

  • Breakfast — any bakery with good Google ratings. Order a café allongé and a croissant. That's Paris.
  • Bouillons ⭐ — authentic Parisian restaurants, generous portions, very affordable. Go early on weekends.
  • Baguette sandwich — from any good bakery. Simple, cheap, perfect.
  • Crêpes — Rue du Montparnasse is the street for this
  • Vietnamese food — Belleville neighbourhood. I love Dong Huong.
  • Falafel — Le Marais, Rue des Rosiers
  • Coffee & Arab pastries ⭐ — Grand Mosque of Paris. Stunning courtyard.

  • Mostly mornings — schedules vary. Make your own picnic: bread, cheese, fruit, wine.
  • Marché des Enfants Rouges ⭐ — more of a food court, amazing options
  • Marché d'Aligre
  • Marché Mouffetard
  • Marché Saint-Germain
  • Marché Saint-Quentin

  • Drinks — Le Marais (wine bars & cocktails) / Canal Saint-Martin (relaxed, local) / Saint-Germain (classic cafés)
  • Party like a local — Oberkampf ⭐ / Pigalle / South Pigalle
  • Jazz — Caveau de la Huchette ⭐ (Quartier Latin, go early, it fills up fast!) / Le Baiser Salé / Duc des Lombards / Le Bal Blomet / Duke's Bar (Thursday & Friday nights, very classy)
